Chickpea, Green Bean, and New Potato Salad With Herb Dressing

Serves 4

Recipe (Loma Linda, California) from "Eat Better Live Longer" By: Melissa Petitto, R.D., pg.43

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces (170 g) haricot verts or green beans
  • 2 pounds (907 g) new potatoes
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 teaspoons (20 ml) fresh lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons (20 g) whole-grain mustard
  • 1/2 cup (78 g) thinly sliced shallots
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 can (15.5 ounces [439 g]) chickpeas, drained and rinsed

Preparation

  1. Bring a medium pot of salted water to a boil. Add the haricot verts and cook for 3 to 4 minutes, or util crisp tender. Remove from the boiling water with a slotted spoon and add to a bowl of ice water.
  2. Add the potatoes to the same pot of boiling water. Reduce the heat to medium and simmer foro 12 to 15 minutes, or until knife tender. Remove from the boiling water with a slottd spoon and allow to cool slightly. Once cool, cut the potatoes in half.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, mustard, shallot, fresh herbs, and salt. Add in the (drained) haricot verts, potatoes, and chickpeas. Gently toss.
  4. Serve warm or cold.
