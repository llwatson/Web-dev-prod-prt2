Wild Greens Pot Pie Recipe

Serves 8

Recipe (Ikaria, Greece) from "Eat Better Live Longer" By: Melissa Petitto, R.D., pg.43

Ingredients

  • 8 cups (4 oz [114 g]) mixed wild greens (dandelion, arugula, spinach, Swiss chard, finally chopped)
  • 1 cup (12 g) mixed tender herbs (dill, parsley, chervil, chives), finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) Greek olive oil, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup (32 g) chopped scallions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon (2g) lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon (15 ml) fresh lemon juice
  • 8 sheets phylio dough, thawed

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C, or gas mark 6)
  2. brush a 9-inch (23-cm) glass plate with olive oil. Set aside
  3. In a large bowl, combine the chopped greens, chopped herbs, and 1/4 cup (60 ml) of the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Massage together to gently wilt the greens, then set aside.
  4. In a small saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon (15 ml) of the olive oil. Once warm, add the scallions and garlic and saute for 1 minute. Add to the wilted greens almond and stir to wilt the greens even further.
  5. Add in the lemon zest and juice and stir to combine.
  6. To assemble the pie, unroll the thawed phylio dough and, while you are working, keep it covered with a moist and clean kitchen towel. working with 1 sheet at a time, lay the phylio dough so that it is centered in the prepared pie plate. Brush thoroughly with about 1 teaspoons olive oil. Repeat with 3 more phylio sheets, for 4 total, brushing with oil in between each layer.
  7. Add the greens mixture to the phylio-lined pie.
  8. To the pie with 4 more layers of phylio, brushing with oil in between each layer and tucking the dough around the greens mixture. Fold the overlapping phylio and roll around the edges to make a nice edge.
  9. Score the phylio dough into 8 even sections, like you'd cut a pie. This will make it easier to cut once it is baked.
  10. Transfer to the oven and bake for 35 to 45 minutes, or until golden brown and puffed. Allow to sit for 10 minutes before sliding out from the pie plate and slicing.
