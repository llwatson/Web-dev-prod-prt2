No-Bake Oatmeal-Raisin Cookies

Makes 18

Recipe (Loma Linda, California) from "Eat Better Live Longer" By: Melissa Petitto, R.D., pg.43

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (250 g) natural creamy almond butter
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) pure maple syrup
  • 1 1/2 cups (220 g) sprouted rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup (75 g) raisins
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a small saucepan over low heat, add the almond butter and maple syrup. Whisk until well incorporated. Heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture begins to bubble. Remove from heat.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the oats, raisins, and cinnamon.
  4. Pour the almond butter mixture over the dry ingredients and use a wooden spoon to combine.
  5. Using a 2-tablespoon (30-ml) cookie scoop, scoop dough and drop onto the prepared baking sheet. Place scoops 2 inches (5 cm) apart.
  6. Flatten cookies with a fork to desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the baking sheet to the freezer and chill for 25 to 30 minutes.
  8. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
